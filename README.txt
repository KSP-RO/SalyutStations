INSTALL
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Unzip into your KSP main folder. Overwrite if you have kosmos already.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BUILDING Station
-------------------------------------------------

Use provided craft files and attach to OLDD proton or Bobcat proton rocket


NOTES
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


-This is an early alpha release. I cannot guarantee everything will work correctly. The weight, aerodynamics and cost of parts may not be ideal.

-The salyut 1 solar arrays are not breakable visibly as they are part of the same model as the station. To try and get around this limitation I made them breakable as an invisible mesh, so if you hit the station
too hard the arrays will lose the ability to collect energy.

-Use the Salyut TKS solar arrays for Salyut 4 and 6 and to convert Salyut 6 to 7, use the Salyut Solar Array with the side extensions.
